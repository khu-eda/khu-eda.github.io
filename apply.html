Research Areas

Applicants are expected to have interest in one or more of the following areas. Prior experience is helpful but not required — motivation and mathematical aptitude matter most.

  • Machine learning for electronic design automation (ML/EDA)
  • Design technology co-optimization (DTCO) and standard cell synthesis
  • Physical design: placement, routing, and parasitic extraction
  • Reinforcement learning for circuit and layout optimization
  • Advanced semiconductor technology: GAA nanosheets, CFET, sub-3nm processes
  • Graph neural networks and neural operators applied to VLSI problems
