Assistant Professor, AIDA Lab

Starting on 1 September 2025, Suwan Kim joins the College of Electronics and Information at Kyung Hee University as an Assistant Professor.

He received B.S. degrees in electrical and computer engineering and naval architecture and ocean engineering from Seoul National University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2019. He completed his Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering from Seoul National University in 2024. He also worked as a staff engineer at the Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology. His research areas include, but are not limited to, machine learning for computer-aided design, design technology co-optimization, and design methodology for advanced nodes.
