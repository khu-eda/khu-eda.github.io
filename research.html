Research

Embedding intelligence into the silicon design flow.

Chip design at advanced nodes is a search over an astronomically large space, run under tight PPA budgets and slow signoff loops. We build models that learn design structure and validate them where it counts: in the full flow, at signoff.

Standard-Cell Design & DTCO

Design-technology co-optimization at the cell level. We synthesize area-optimal CFET standard cells through optimal transistor folding and placement, design multi-skewed multi-bit flip-flop cells for useful-skew timing optimization, and boost pin accessibility through cell layout diversification and replacement.

ML-driven Physical Design

Prediction models that make physical design converge faster. We developed pin accessibility-driven placement optimization, DRC hotspot prediction and resolution built on graph neural networks and U-Net, and ML-based capacitance extraction for parasitic estimation and interconnect geometry exploration.

AI for Analog Circuit Design

Learning-based analog design automation. We optimize transistor sizing with model-based reinforcement learning and TDA-guided landscape-aware search, and learn analog circuit representations with domain-aware hypergraph transformers.

Design methodologies for emerging technologies. We build design enablement for CFET and physical design flows for monolithic 3D ICs, including 3D via allocation tightly linked with routing optimization.
