About the Lab

The AI-aided Design Automation (AIDA) Lab is a research group at Kyung Hee University (Yongin, Korea), led by Prof. Suwan Kim. We work at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and electronic design automation (EDA), building intelligent algorithms that automate and accelerate semiconductor design.

Our mission is to reduce design cycles and improve power-performance-area (PPA) tradeoffs by embedding AI/ML into every stage of the EDA flow.

We collaborate closely with industry and leading research groups, targeting advanced algorithms in machine learning and AI.
